# Custom Domains for Creator Storefronts

Creators selling digital products want **shop.creatorname.com**, not **yourplatform.com/creatorname/shop**.

When a creator directs their audience to buy their course, templates, or digital products, they want that purchase happening on their brand, not yours. Custom domains make your platform invisible while powering their entire business.

## The Problem

Gumroad, Payhip, and Sellfy all support custom domains for the same reason: creators who are serious about their business won't settle for a branded checkout experience from someone else's platform.

When a creator sells a $500 course at **yourplatform.com/creator/checkout**, they're trusting you with their brand reputation. But they also know they don't control that URL. If they ever leave your platform, all their payment links break.
